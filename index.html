About

Professional Profile

Joe P. Samples is an accomplished researcher with extensive experience in neurobiology, cell and molecular biology, and respiratory biology. With a Ph.D. in Neuroscience and a strong background in teaching and research, Joe has contributed significantly to the understanding of developmental phenomena and regeneration in higher vertebrates. His technical expertise includes RT-PCR, confocal microscopy, and tissue culture, among others.
